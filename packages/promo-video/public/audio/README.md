# Promo video audio assets

- `vo-*.m4a` - per-beat voiceover generated by
  `scripts/generate-elevenlabs-voiceover.mjs` with ElevenLabs TTS (requires
  `ELEVENLABS_API_KEY` in `.env.elevenlabs.local` at the repo root). The
  earlier `scripts/generate-openai-voiceover.mjs` (OpenAI TTS) and the
  offline `scripts/generate-voiceover.zsh` (macOS `say`) remain as fallbacks.
- `music.mp3` - "Travel Promo" by The_Mountain, downloaded from Pixabay:
  https://pixabay.com/music/main-title-travel-promo-158075/
  The track is listed as free for use under the Pixabay Content License.
