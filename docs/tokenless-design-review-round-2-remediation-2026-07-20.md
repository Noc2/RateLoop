# Tokenless design review, round 2 — remediation record

**Status:** Internal source-remediation record for the findings in
[Tokenless design review, round 2 — 20 July 2026](tokenless-design-review-2026-07-20.md). Each finding was checked
against the current `tokenless` source before changing it and checked again after its focused fix. A source fix is not
evidence that a provider, live-operations, economics, legal, external-audit, or deployment gate has passed. The
[production-readiness register](tokenless-production-readiness-2026-07.md) remains the release checklist.

## Carried findings

The round-two review re-verified several round-one findings that are deliberately contained rather than closed. They
remain release blockers: source-derived adaptive safety gates and platform-synthetic gold (W1/W2), the near-unanimous
surprise-bounty incentive (W9), cross-round sybil/collusion analytics (W11), under-reporting detection for advisory
integrations, reviewer economics, and the preregistered real-money acceptance review. This remediation does not
convert those mechanism, economics, or live-operations questions into source-code claims.

## Verification rule

The focused tests named above are necessary regression evidence. Release approval additionally requires the complete
package/type/Foundry verification matrix, the current ordered migration journal, exact deployment identity, managed
resource evidence, and the live paid-path and operations exercises in the readiness register.
